## Sensors
This page explains the purpose and specifications of each sensor.

# DHT11 ()
- Measures temperature and humidity
- Voltage Requirement of 3-5V. It can operate at a lower voltage but is less reliable. Recommended voltage is 5V.
- 3 Pin (Signal, Power, Ground)
- Older model compared to the DHT22
- This module cannot provide accurate data (Around +- 5%)
- Update time of 2 seconds (1Hz)
